Plain and Simple Sourdough Bread

Simple sourdough bread for the bread machine.

Adding a few simple ingredients will create a plain and simple sourdough bread.

Ingredients:

  • ¾ cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 cup sourdough starter
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 2 ⅔ cups bread flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients in order suggested by your manufacturer.
  2. Select white bread setting and push start.
